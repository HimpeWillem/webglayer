Visualization made by WebGLayer
Parallel Coordinates
The app visualizes 63 532 traffic accidents that happened in Flanders in the recent years. The data was provided by the Federal Police and processed in collabration with Informatie Vlaanderen. Filter the data directly by zooming, sketching polygons or in charts in the right panel. Data patterns and relationships can be discovered in the parallel coordinates panel. Please refer to the user guide for more tips on the app use and data interpretation.
